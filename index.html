Monika Jaskolka, PhD

Currently, I work for Fiat Chrysler Automobiles (FCA) on the Software Methods and Tools team, where I am primarily focused on establishing and executing best practices for model-based development workflows for automotive embedded controls software. I develop tools to support these efforts. I also work on Simulink model optimization and refactoring.

I completed my PhD in Software Engineering at McMaster University under the supervision of Dr. Mark Lawford and Dr. Alan Wassyng. My research focussed on the model-based development of embedded software using the Similink language. I was a recipient of the NSERC Alexander Graham Bell Canada Graduate Scholarship - Doctoral (CGS D), which provided $70,000CAD towards my research. I also developed several tools that are maintained on the McSCert GitHub page.
